Have An Adventure

Make Memories with Dry Oar

The Main Fork of the Salmon River in Idaho, known as the "River of No Return," captivates visitors with its rugged beauty. Elk, deer, bears, and bighorn sheep are commonly spotted, while ponderosa pines offer shelter along sandy beaches. The river features rapids ranging from class I-IV, and as one of the longest undammed rivers in the US, its water levels vary from high and fast in early summer to low and slow in late summer.

The Grand Canyon in Arizona offers the ultimate river adventure, combining world-class whitewater and awe-inspiring scenery. This iconic destination features rapids ranging from Class I to X, with side canyons and ancient Indian ruins accessible only by river. Whether you're navigating thrilling rapids or exploring hidden treasures, a trip to the Grand Canyon promises an unforgettable experience that will leave a lasting impression.

Desolation and Gray Canyon in central Utah offer a captivating 84-mile journey along the Green River. Featuring stunning desert landscapes, towering cliffs, and exhilarating rapids, this six-day adventure is rated Class II/III. Discover fascinating geological formations and evidence of human history in this National Landmark, making it a must-visit destination for those seeking remarkable whitewater opportunities and rich historical experiences.

Our whitewater rafting trip addresses common concerns:

1. Suitability for Kids: Children of all ages are welcome, with those over 8 typically enjoying it more. Safety is ensured with certified guides and captivating scenery.

2. Measures: Guides are trained in first aid, and safety talks prepare participants for emergencies. Mandatory life vests are provided for all.

3. Inclusions: The trip includes camping gear, meals, and limited beverages. Participants can bring a reasonable amount of alcohol.

4. Trip Highlights: Rafting offers adventure, scenic beauty, hikes, history, and delicious meals for unforgettable experiences.

5. Booking Recommendations: Booking in advance is recommended, with specific dates advised a year ahead and flexibility allowing six months.

In summary, the FAQ assures a thrilling and safe adventure suitable for all ages, with comprehensive provisions and memorable experiences.
