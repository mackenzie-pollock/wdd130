Welcome to Our Wakeboarding & Wake Surfing Tours

About Us

Welcome to our wakesurfing tour in Yuma, AZ, along the Colorado River! Join our family for an unforgettable boating experience. Whether you're a wakeboarding pro, eager to learn wakesurfing, or simply enjoy tubing, we've got you covered. Our expert instructors provide personalized lessons for all skill levels. Come and enjoy the thrill of the water against the backdrop of Arizona's sun-soaked landscapes. Laugh, learn, and create memories with us as we navigate the stunning waters of Yuma. It's an adventure you won't soon forget!

Our Tours

Senators Wash Adventure

Experience the teal waters and warm sunshine of the Colorado River as you wakeboard and wake surf through the desert oasis.

Lake Powel Adventure

Embark on an exhilarating wakeboarding daytime adventure amidst the breathtaking scenery of Lake Powell. Feel the rush as you carve through crystal-clear waters surrounded by towering red rock cliffs. With expert guides and top-notch equipment, experience the ultimate thrill on this unforgettable Lake Powell wakeboarding day tour.

Park Moabi Adventure

Dive into the excitement of wakeboarding at Park Moabi along the Colorado River! Glide across the river's serene waters, framed by stunning desert landscapes. Expert instruction and pristine conditions ensure an unforgettable adventure.
