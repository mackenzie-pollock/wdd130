Info

In the arid expanse of Yuma, Arizona, where shimmering lakes offer refuge from the desert heat, the genesis of our wakeboarding and wakesurfing tour company began. Founded by a family bonded by a shared love for water sports, our company emanates values of passion, safety, and adventure. With a dedication to providing unforgettable experiences, we've crafted tours to the breathtaking waters of Lake Powell, the serene allure of Park Moabi, and the tranquil vistas of Senators Wash.

Our team of experienced tour guides, led by Justin Pollock, Mackenzie Pollock, Liz Pollock, and Grayson Pollock, embodies our commitment to excellence. Drawing upon their extensive knowledge of the waters and a genuine enthusiasm for sharing their expertise, our guides ensure every excursion is filled with excitement and memories to cherish. From beginners to seasoned enthusiasts, we welcome all to embark on an exhilarating journey with us, where the thrill of the waves meets the warmth of familial hospitality.
